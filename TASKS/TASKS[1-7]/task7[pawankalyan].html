FILM INDUSTRY CAREER
  • Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi.
  • In 2008, Jalsa, directed by Trivikram Srinivas and produced by Allu Aravind was released on 2 April. This film got the highest first-day collection in Telugu film history and was also the first for any regional film in south India in a single state by that time
  • He acted in the film Kushi. The film was released on 27 April 2001 and was directed by S. J. Suryah.
  • In 2014, a Star India survey ranked Kalyan as one of the Top 5 Heroes of India.
POLITICAL CAREER
  • Kalyan started his political career in 2008[52] as the president of Yuvarajyam,[53] the youth wing of Praja Rajyam Party which was started by his elder brother Chiranjeevi.
  • In 2019, Pawan Kalyan contested from two assembly constituencies -- Gajuwaka and Bhimavaram but lost both. In the face of a YSRCP wave at that time, the Jana Sena Party managed to get only 6% vote share. At that time Pawan Kalyan allied with the CPI, CPM and the Bahujan Samaj Party.
  • Film star Pawan Kalyan has finally made it. Head of Jana Sena Party (JSP) was sworn in as a minister today in Andhra Pradesh assembly along with Naidu. There are speculations that Pawan Kalyan will take the role of deputy chief minister. Kalyan's party was part of the TDP-BJP alliance and won 21 assembly and 2 Lok Sabha seats.
Details

Pawan Kalyan is one of the most famous Telugu actors as well as a director and screenwriter / choregrapher. His actual name is Konidela Babu, and he changed his name to Pawan Kalyan after the movie Akkada Abbai Ikkada Ammayi. His elder brother was an actor-politician, Chiranjeevi. Even Chiranjeevi was there in his debut film in 1996 named 'Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi'. In 1998 his movie Tholi Prema won National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu. He is popular as a "Power Star" among his fans; Kalyan is known for his movies such as Gokram Rosita, Swagatam, Toriprema, Tamdu, Badri, Kushi, Jarsa, Gabersin, Goparagopara, and Atalinticida Lady. He won the Best Actor Filmfare Award-Gabbar Singh's Telugu version; Attarintiki Daredi held the record for the highest-selling Telugu film.
